Smart Home LTL is a unique web product developed by a group of students at the University of Technical Education. This product gives users the ability to control home devices conveniently and intelligently.


With Smart Home LTL, you can control lights, air conditioners, watering systems, and many other devices with just a few clicks. Furthermore, the product also connects to sensors to collect environmental data, helping you monitor and adjust factors such as temperature, humidity, light, and indoor air quality.


Smart Home LTL not only makes life more convenient but also saves energy and contributes to environmental protection. This is the perfect combination of technology and everyday life. Let's experience Smart Home to turn your home into a truly smart home!
